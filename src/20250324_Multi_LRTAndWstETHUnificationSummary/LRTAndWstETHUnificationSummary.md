## Motivation

This publication consolidates recent amendments proposed to various ETH, LST and LRT parameter updates across Aave v3.

- Creating/updating Liquid eModes (wstETH/WETH, weETH/WETH, rsETH/WETH, ezETH/WETH) on Core, Prime, Arbitrum, and Base instances.
- Revising LTV/LT values for select LSTs and LRTs,
- Increasing Supply Caps.

### Liquid eMode v3.2 Updates

The introduction of Liquid eModes in Aave v3.2 enables more granular and targeted risk configurations between correlated assets such as LSTs and LRTs. Creating isolated eModes for each pair enhances capital efficiency relative to the current ETH-correlated E-Modes on Core market.

This update allows for better-aligned risk parameters based on the individual performance of each asset pair. The following Liquid eModes to be created are:

- rsETH/WETH on Core.
- ezETH/WETH on Core.
- ezETH/wstETH on Core.
- weETH/WETH on Core.
- weETH/WETH on Arbitrum.
- weETH/WETH on Base.

Reference: [[ARFC] wstETH and weETH E-Modes and LT/LTV Adjustments on Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base - 03.12.25](https://governance.aave.com/t/arfc-wsteth-and-weeth-e-modes-and-lt-ltv-adjustments-on-ethereum-arbitrum-base-03-12-25/21370)

### wstETH/WETH eMode Update

After the initial proposal supported creating wstETH collateral and ETH debt eModes across Ethereum, Arbitrum and Base, a second publication proposed increasing the wstETH LTV and LT parameters. Increasing the LTV and LT enables users to take on more leverage and amplifies the yield generated by arbitraging Aave Protocol's ETH funding rate and Lido's wstETH Index Rate.

Favorable eMode terms led to significant growth on the Prime instance and a modest increase in its LTV (to 93.5%) and LT (to 96%) has been shown to materially boost APY, making this an impactful yet low-risk adjustment.

wstETH/WETH liquid eMode on Core, Prime, Arbitrum and Base instances:

- Increase eMode LTV 93.5% and LT 96%.

This change have been approved by Risk Service Providers.

Reference: [[Risk Stewards]wstETH/wETH eMode Update - Ethereum, Arbitrum & Base Instances](https://governance.aave.com/t/risk-stewards-wsteth-weth-emode-update-ethereum-arbitrum-base-instances/21333)

### rsETH/WETH eMode and Supply Cap Increase

To promote a level playing field between LRTs, a proposal was submitted and approved by Risk Service Providers to align the LTV and LT parameters of rsETH with other LRTs, ezETH and weETH.

To support the continued growth of rsETH, a rsETH collateral and WETH debt eMode combined with a Supply Cap was proposed for the Core instance via a separate publication.

The following is proposed for rsETH Core, Prime and Base instances:

- Update rsETH/wstETH eMode: LTV 93%, LT 95% and Liquidation Penalty 1%.
- Create rsETH/WETH eMode.
- Increase Supply cap by +70,000 on Core.

Creating a new rsETH/WETH eMode on Core and increasing the Supply Cap increase is pending Risk Service Provider input. The LTV and LT updates have been approved by Risk Service Providers.

Reference: [[ARFC] rsETH LTV & LT Update](https://governance.aave.com/t/arfc-rseth-ltv-lt-update/21305/1)

### Add ezETH to Core

ezETH is currently deployed on multiple Aave instances without the ability to borrow ETH. Onboarding ezETH to Core with an ezETH collateral and WETH debt eMode is expected to stimulate demand for ETH. Coupled with the existing ezETH/wstETH eMode, this will enhance user flexibility and further integrate the token into the Aave ecosystem.

The following is proposed for ezETH:

- Add ezETH to Core instance.
- Create ezETH/WETH and ezETH/wstETH eModes on Core.

This publication proposed onboarding ezETH to Core instance using the Direct-to-AIP process.

The proposal is pending Risk Service Provider input.

Reference: [[ARFC] Core Instance - Add ezETH and update rsETH eMode Parameters](https://governance.aave.com/t/arfc-core-instance-add-ezeth-and-update-rseth-emode-parameters/21505)

### Systemic Benefits and Ecosystem Alignment

These updates will improve overall capital efficiency across Aave, drive higher ETH utilization, and support the growth of protocols such as KelpDAO and Renzo through better access to staked ETH liquidity.

The wstETH LTV and LT increase reflects the Aave DAO's alignment with Lido through the Lido Alliance, by reflecting wstETHâ€™s superior risk profile of wtETH across Aave Protocol.
